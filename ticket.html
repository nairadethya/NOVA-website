Astrophotography competitions

Are you someone who have a passion for capturing the wonders of space and have an eye for capturing the perfect shot..Then it's time to gear up, point your cameras to the sky and click your Astro-Photograph!.We welcome your submissions and look forward to seeing the beauty of the universe through your lens

Reels making competitions:

Are you an astronomy enthusiast who has a passion for astronomy and a creative eye for video production. This is your chance to showcase your talent and share your love for the universe with others.So grab your camera and start filming the universe!

Meme making competitions:

If you are someone who loves astronomy and have a knack for creating hilarious and creative memes,this is your chance to showcase your creativity and love for astronomy while having some fun. !Be creative and let your passion for astronomy shine through!

Just a minute :

Are you ready for an exciting game of astronomy trivia? In this fast-paced game, participants will be challenged to talk about a given astronomy topic for one minute without hesitation, deviation or repetition.This is a great opportunity to showcase your astronomy knowledge, learn new facts and meet other astronomy enthusiasts

Treasure Hunt :

You will have the opportunity to participate in a fun and challenging treasure hunt that will test your knowledge of astronomy and take you on a journey through the stars.This is a great opportunity to meet other astronomy enthusiasts and to learn more

Astrophotography and star gazing workshops:

Attention all stargazers and astrophotographers! Join us for an immersive experience where you will learn the art of astrophotography and how to capture stunning images of the night sky.Our experts will take you on a journey through the constellations, planets, and deep sky objects, sharing their knowledge and passion for astronomy
