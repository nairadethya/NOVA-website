About Us

NOVA 3.0

Promo

Nova 3.0 is a collaborative venture featuring space enthusiasts and communities from leading colleges of the state which shall see active participation from all over Kerala. Nova was first introduced in 2020 as a part of the International Space Week by "Pioneers" , a collective of space enthusiasts from all over Kerala which was aimed at inspiring students from all disciplines to pursue their interest in Space Research and Development.

This initiative focuses on promoting space research and exploration as a bright career option and promises to offer an unparalleled opportunity to learn from industry experts, network with peers, and explore the latest developments in space technology.

Inaugration and Key Note Address By

Padma Shri M C Dathan

Indian Space Scientist

M.C. Dathan is a renowned Indian space scientist who has made significant contributions to the development of India's space program. He has served in various capacities at the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), and is best known for his role as the director of the Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre (VSSC) from 2007 to 2014. During his tenure at VSSC, Dathan oversaw the development and launch of several important satellites, including the PSLV C25 mission that sent India's Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM) spacecraft into orbit around Mars. This mission was a major achievement for india's space program, and helped to establish the country as a serious player in the global space industry. Dathan has received many awards and honors for his contributions to science and technology, including the Padma Shri in 2013. He continues to be an influential figure in the Indian scientific community, and his work has inspired many young people to pursue careers in space science and technology

Our Venue

Government Engineering College Thrissur - 680 009, Kerala, India

Government Engineering College Trichur, is one of the most prestigious and reputed technical education institution in south India. The College was established in the year 1957. The foundation of the building was laid down by Honorable Prime Minister, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. The college is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and it is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Council of Architecture (COA) and programmes are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). The campus of GEC Thrissur is spread over an area of more than 75 acres of land.
